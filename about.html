Our Mission

DIM Won, LLC is a private holding company dedicated to building sustainable wealth through strategic investments and long-term asset management. Founded with a focus on autonomy, privacy, and impact, we actively grow our portfolio while preserving the integrity of each venture we undertake.

What We Do

We invest in a diverse range of opportunities, including real estate, dividend-yielding stocks, and private ventures such as professional sports ownership and digital infrastructure. Every investment is backed by thorough analysis and a long-term vision.

Our Structure

DIM Won, LLC is the parent entity to multiple subsidiaries. Our primary operating company, DIM Real Estate Investments, LLC, focuses on acquiring and revitalizing distressed properties across Georgia and beyond.
