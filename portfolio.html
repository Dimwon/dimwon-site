Portfolio

Diversified ownership across asset classes.

DIM Won invests across asset classes that support long-term value creation. Our portfolio is constructed with patience and concentrated where we have conviction. We are owners first — we do not trade positions, and we do not pursue strategies we do not understand.

Public Markets

Equities with stable economics, durable franchises, and the capacity to compound retained earnings over the long term.

Real Estate

Residential and commercial holdings selected for cash-flow stability, location quality, and structural demand over multi-decade horizons.

Private Ventures

Strategic ownership stakes and operating partnerships in private businesses with intelligible economics and a clear path to durability.

A note on disclosure. As a private holding company, DIM Won, LLC does not publish a complete list of holdings. Specific portfolio companies may be added to this page as our investments grow and disclosure becomes appropriate.
